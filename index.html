How It Works

  • The simulator randomly selects one of 6200 stocks from the S&P 500, with dates and names removed to prevent them from influencing your trading decisions.
  • Feel free to use any combination of indicators to guide your trading choices.
  • Click 'Add 1 day(s)' to move the chart forward by a day, or adjust the increment to add multiple days with each click.
  • When buying or selling, your entire cash balance is used for purchasing, and all shares of the stock are sold.
  • A 0.25% trading fee applies to each transaction on the fictional balance.
  • If the current stock data seems unclear or you wish to explore another stock, simply click to choose a different one.
